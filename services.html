Core Services

Deep-dive capability for critical business risks.

We provide authoritative assurance for sensitive situations, disputes and regulatory challenges — each engagement scoped under NDA and delivered as clear, factual findings.

01

Corporate Investigations

Discreet corporate investigations into fraud, misconduct or operational anomalies requiring evidence. We establish the facts objectively, preserving evidential integrity throughout, so that findings can support internal decisions, disciplinary action or legal proceedings.

Every investigation is scoped to be necessary, justified and proportionate to the risk — nothing more.

Fraud & misconductEvidence preservationInterview supportLegally sound reporting
02

Business Risk & Resilience Review

Comprehensive assessment of operational vulnerabilities and resilience planning for board-level assurance. We map exposure across people, process and technology, then translate it into a clear, prioritised picture the board can act on.

Vulnerability assessmentResilience planningBoard-level reportingRisk prioritisation
03

Sensitive Data Exfiltration & Discovery

Identification of unauthorised data transfers and recovery of sensitive intellectual property. Where confidential information has left the organisation — or is suspected to have — we determine what moved, how, and to whom, with defensible evidence.

Data loss investigationIP recoveryExfiltration analysisChain of custody
04

Device Forensics

Forensically sound acquisition and analysis of data from mobile devices and computers. Using validated methods, we recover, preserve and interpret digital evidence in a manner designed to withstand legal and regulatory scrutiny.

Mobile & computerSound acquisitionDeleted data recoveryExpert analysis

Incident Response

Rapid deployment to manage, contain and investigate critical security or data incidents. When time matters, we move quickly to limit impact, preserve evidence, and establish exactly what happened — coordinating with your legal and compliance teams throughout.

Rapid deploymentContainmentBreach investigationRegulatory coordination

Compliance Assistance — GDPR / ICO

Data protectionICO reportingBreach notificationPractical guidance

Technical fact-finding to support HR and legal teams in complex employee disputes. We provide impartial, evidence-based analysis that helps sensitive matters be resolved fairly and defensibly.
